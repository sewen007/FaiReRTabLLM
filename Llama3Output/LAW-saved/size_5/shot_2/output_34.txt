Here is a Python script that can rank the applicants based on their UGPA and LSAT scores. This script will rank the applicants based on their total score which is the sum of their UGPA and LSAT scores.

```Python
def rank_applicants(applicants):
    ranked_applicants = sorted(applicants, key=lambda x: (x['UGPA'] + x['LSAT']), reverse=True)
    return [{student_id: applicant['student_id'], gender: applicant['gender']} for applicant in ranked_applicants]

applicants = [
    {'student_id': 9621, 'UGPA': 3.2, 'LSAT': 32.5, 'gender': 'female'},
    {'student_id': 19568, 'UGPA': 3.5, 'LSAT': 37.0, 'gender': 'female'},
    {'student_id': 11597, 'UGPA': 2.8, 'LSAT': 35.0, 'gender': 'female'},
    {'student_id': 18163, 'UGPA': 3.4, 'LSAT': 37.0, 'gender':'male'},
    {'student_id': 21028, 'UGPA': 3.2, 'LSAT': 32.3, 'gender':'male'}
]

print(rank_applicants(applicants))
```
